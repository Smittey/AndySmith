The Planck - Key Theory

Part 2, How I approached and adapted the layout of the Planck

August 25th, 2018

Keyboards DIY

I’ve written a lot about Mechanical keyboards, and if it wasn’t already clear, I’m somewhat of an enthusiast. This hobby and interest exposes you to a range of keyboards that you didn’t previously know existed with an array of layouts, sizes, and colours. I became particularly intrigued by tiny keyboards as they not only look weird, but supposedly can help with both typing efficiency and RSI. After many months of browsing enthusiast forums I came across a Planck. Looking down at your keyboard now you’ll notice that the layout is staggered, meaning the keys aren’t all aligned. The Planck on the other hand is ‘Ortholinear’; the keys are in a columnar fashion! Weird. I like it!
